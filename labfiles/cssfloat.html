1. float: left

Float Left

This paragraph demonstrates how text flows around an element that is floated to the left. The floated box allows the text to wrap around its right side. Float is useful for aligning images beside content.

2. float: right

Float Right

This paragraph shows floating to the right. Text wraps around the left side. Floats were commonly used in older website layouts before flexbox and grid.
